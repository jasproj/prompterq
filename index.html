Show-Day Workflow

From dirty Word doc to glass-ready script.

The value isn't just scrolling. It's cutting script-prep time and reducing live output risk. The same workflow, every time. No surprises at call time.

1

Import

Drop in TXT, RTF, or DOCX. PrompterQ normalizes spacing, bullets, smart quotes, page numbers, and line breaks automatically.

2

Format

One click. ¶ FMT runs speech-rhythm line breaks. Curly quotes, em-dashes, encoding garbage — gone.

3

Mark

Speaker colors (Ctrl+1/2/3), italics for emphasis (Ctrl+I), and 36 cue markers (Ctrl+M). Save the show as a .pqs session.

4

Prompt

F11 to fullscreen. M to mirror. Arrows for speed. PageUp/Down to switch scripts. You're rolling.
